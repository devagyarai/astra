# Astra v1.0.0 Release Notes

We are thrilled to announce the official release of **Astra v1.0.0** — The AI-powered Decision Intelligence Platform.

## What is Astra?
Astra transforms the way you make decisions. Instead of relying on scattered notes, disconnected documents, and fragmented thoughts, Astra provides a unified, structured Decision Canvas. With the introduction of **Astra Copilot**, the platform now actively guides your thinking, detecting biases, analyzing trade-offs, and suggesting actionable goals in real-time.

## Key Features

- **Astra Copilot:** Your personal AI decision partner. Integrates directly into your canvas to provide intelligent insights, bias detection, and alternative perspectives.
- **BYOK (Bring Your Own Key):** Enterprise-grade security out of the box. Your data, your API keys (OpenAI / Anthropic). Keys never leave your browser's local storage.
- **Structured Decision Canvas:** Methodically outline your Goals, Context, Constraints, Options, and Evidence.
- **Offline-First Architecture:** Write, brainstorm, and strategize from anywhere. All decisions are instantly persisted locally without requiring an active internet connection.

We’ve spent the RC phases polishing the UX, hardening the security boundaries, and maximizing performance. Astra is now ready for production use.

## Getting Started
Just open the application, head to Settings to securely add your API key, and create your first Decision. Welcome to clarity.
